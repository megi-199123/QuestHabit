Privacy Policy for Quest Habit

Last updated: March 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Quest Habit ("we", "us", or "our") handles your information when you use our mobile application Quest Habit (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Character name — a name you choose to personalize your in-app story. This is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to our servers.

Information Collected Automatically

  • App activity — quest completions, habit streaks, and story progress. This data is stored locally on your device and is used solely to provide app functionality.
  • AI-generated content requests — when you trigger story generation, anonymized gameplay data (quest completion percentage, genre selection, story context) is sent to our AI provider (Anthropic) to generate your narrative. No personally identifiable information is included in these requests.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to:

  • Provide and personalize the core app experience (habit tracking, story generation)
  • Generate your daily story snippets, weekly episodes, and monthly chapters
  • Maintain your streak and progress history locally on your device

3. Data Storage

All user data (quests, habits, story journal, settings) is stored locally on your device using an on-device database. We do not operate servers that store your personal data.

4. Third-Party Services

Quest Habit uses the following third-party service:

Service Purpose Privacy Policy
Anthropic Claude API AI story generation anthropic.com/privacy

When story generation is triggered, anonymized context data is sent to Anthropic's API. This data does not include your real name, device identifiers, or any personally identifiable information.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with any third parties except as described in Section 4 (AI story generation).

6. Children's Privacy

Quest Habit is intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

7. Data Retention

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all app data at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing app data in your device settings.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
